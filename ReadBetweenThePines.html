Read Between the Pines

Are your bags packed and ready for an extended trip to Havenwood, Washington?

Evie Shaw is determined to leave her past behind, including a disappointing life and an unfaithful fiancé. After discovering his affair with her best friend, she cuts all ties and starts anew. With a charming new home, a job she loves, and an unexpectedly supportive online follower, Evie is ready for a fresh start. But little does she know, a secret admirer is watching her every move…

Samuel Lawson has loved Evie since childhood. Once inseparable, a series of unfortunate events drove them apart. For years, his only connection to her was through her online streams, hidden behind an alias. Those close to Evie ensured it was his only option. Now, a decade later, with the obstacles that once kept them apart finally gone, Sam is free to make his move. He will stop at nothing to rekindle their bond… no matter what it takes.
