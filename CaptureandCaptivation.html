Capture and Captivation

In the neon-lit labyrinth of Dagmar-1, Vexauna Strange fights to survive as a bounty hunter in crime ridden streets. But mere survival isn’t enough for Vexauna. She craves more—a life beyond the shadows.

Fate intervenes when an irresistible contract lands in her lap; hunt down the enigmatic gangster, Nova Kane. With pressure mounting the mission takes an unexpected turn and Vex finds herself having to outrun her target. Now Kane and a mysterious figure from the shadows are hot on her trail. In a city where loyalty is a currency and love can be a fatal weakness, Vexauna must choose: sacrifice her heart or potentially lose everything she holds dear.
