Cabin Fever

Three friends. One cabin. Zero chill.

Rosie, Alex, and Joie have been inseparable since elementary school. But after years apart—college, grad school, life—they reunite for a cozy winter getaway in a remote mountain Airbnb. What starts as a nostalgic trip quickly turns into a pressure cooker of long-held secrets and unresolved feelings.

When a game turns into a challenge with Rosie as the prize, the trio must confront their desires, their loyalties, and the messy truth about what they really want.

Cabin Fever is a spicy, chaotic, and emotionally charged short story about friendship, love, and the games we play when we’re finally ready to stop pretending.
