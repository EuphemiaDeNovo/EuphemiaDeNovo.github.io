About the Author

Euphemia (Effie) De Novo is an author with a penchant for self-indulgent and delightfully outlandish romances. When she’s not weaving tales of passion and intrigue, you’ll find her walking around her favorite neighborhoods in her city or immersed in video games. But Effie’s true sanctuary lies by the beach, where she scours the sand for as much beach glass as she can fill her pockets with. Beyond her day job, Effie discovered solace within the vibrant romance writing community. Driven by a desire to contribute her unique voice to the genre, she embarked on her literary journey.

Want to stay in the loop? Follow Effie on Instagram, TikTok or Amazon to receive updates on her latest book releases, or sign up for her newsletter down below.

Writen Works

  • Capture and Captivation

    Triggers and Tropes:

    * Stalking

    * Found family

    * Science fiction

    * He falls first and harder

    * Talk me through it

    * Happily ever after

    In the neon-lit labyrinth of Dagmar-1, Vexauna Strange fights to survive as a bounty hunter in crime ridden streets. But mere survival isn’t enough for Vexauna. She craves more—a life beyond the shadows. Fate intervenes when an irresistible contract lands in her lap; hunt down the enigmatic gangster, Nova Kane. With pressure mounting the mission takes an unexpected turn and Vex finds herself having to outrun her target. Now Kane and a mysterious figure from the shadows are hot on her trail. In a city where loyalty is a currency and love can be a fatal weakness, Vexauna must choose: sacrifice her heart or potentially lose everything she holds dear.
