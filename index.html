About the Author

Euphemia (Effie) De Novo is an author with a penchant for self-indulgent and delightfully outlandish romances. When she’s not weaving tales of passion and intrigue, you’ll find her walking around her favorite neighborhoods in her city or immersed in video games. But Effie’s true sanctuary lies by the beach, where she scours the sand for precious beach glass—a hobby that mirrors the hidden treasures she unearths in her writing. Beyond her day job, Effie discovered solace within the vibrant romance writing community. Driven by a desire to contribute her unique voice to the genre, she embarked on her literary journey. Now, her words dance across pages, inviting readers to escape into love stories that defy convention.

Want to stay in the loop? Follow Effie on Instagram, TikTok or Amazon to receive updates on her latest book releases, or sign up for her newsletter down below.

Writen Works

  • Capture and Captivation

    In the neon-lit labyrinth of Dagmar-1, Vexauna Strange fights to survive. Her days blur into nights as she stalks notorious criminals through dark, gritty streets. But mere survival isn’t enough for Vexauna. She craves more—a life beyond the shadows, where the neon glow doesn’t sear her soul. Fate intervenes when an irresistible contract lands in her lap; hunt down the enigmatic gangster, Nova Kane. With pressure mounting the mission takes an unexpected turn and Vex finds herself having to outrun her targets. Now Kane and a mysterious figure from the shadows are hot on her trail. In a city where loyalty is a currency and love can be a fatal weakness, Vexauna must choose: sacrifice her heart or potentially lose everything she holds dear.

  • The Alchemy of Waves and Saltwater

    Coming June 30th

    Sofie Carter has fled her painful past, leaving behind an abusive family. In the quaint seaside village of Pacifica Bay, Washington, she finds solace—a new apartment, a fresh start, and the promise of the life she’s always dreamed of. But fate intervenes when she has an unexpected ‘meet cute’ with Jessie, the town hermit—an enigmatic man who lives in the shadow of his own painful past. As Sofie grapples with newfound desires, her family’s unwelcome intrusion threatens to unravel everything she’s built.
